Community Fridge Resources

This semester I've been working with a group of 4 other students to create a project interest technology project. We interviewed many community fridge organizers throughout New York City and discovered that there was no central hub to share resources, despite this being viewed as helpful. To fill this gap, we created a web app that lets users access contact templates, a forum for tips, and guides created by organizers. I did a large portion of the coding for the web application on both the front and back end.
